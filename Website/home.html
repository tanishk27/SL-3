Don't Know Much About Anime? Don't Worry! Here is some Info for You!!

Anime is hand-drawn and computer animation originating from Japan. The term Anime is derived from the English word animation, and in Japan is used to refer to all forms of animated media. As a multimedia art form, it combines graphic art, characterization, cinematography, and other creative techniques. Anime is generally targeted towards more older audience and has various Genres, where as Cartoon is more kids oriented and mostly focused on Comedy.
